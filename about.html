About Pass The Pages

A student-to-student book donation and exchange platform

Our Story

Pass The Pages was built by students, for students — with the mission of reducing textbook waste and making learning more accessible. We noticed how many books were sitting unused on shelves while others couldn’t afford to buy them. This platform aims to bridge that gap.

Our Mission

  • 📚 Encourage sustainable book sharing among students
  • 💸 Save students money on expensive textbooks
  • ♻️ Promote reuse and reduce waste on campus
  • 🧑‍🤝‍🧑 Foster a helpful, student-driven community

Meet the Team
